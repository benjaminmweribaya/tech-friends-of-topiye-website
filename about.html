About us

We are a social enterprise dedicated to transforming the lives of youth through hands-on skill training, with a mission to eliminate poverty, combat drug abuse, reduce gender-based violence, address insecurities, and, most importantly, tackle climate change.

Our Approach

TVET Green Initiative

This program empowers communities to protect the environment by distributing free tree seedlings and raising awareness about the importance of environmental conservation. It aims to restore nature and encourage sustainable practices to protect our planet.

TFT Mentorship Program

This initiative aims to shift youth attitudes towards hands-on skill training, recognizing that the global economy is increasingly driven by skilled labor. By investing in our youth, we cultivate agents of change who can drive sustainable development.

Innovative Projects

Smart Climate Stool (ekicholong)

The Smart Climate Stool (ekicholong) is invented by TFT GRIT Workshop CEO Loroo. Made from scrap metal, this stool combats deforestation by providing an alternative to traditional wooden ekicholong. In Turkana County alone, this initiative aims to save over 500,000 trees, traditionally used to make ekicholong, by offering a durable, metal alternative.

The adoption of the metal ekicholong is spreading to neighboring communities, including the Pokot and Teso of Kenya, the Karimojong of Uganda, and the Nyangatom and Merile of Ethiopia, as well as the Toposa and Dongiro of South Sudan. The commercialization of ekicholong as gifts has exacerbated deforestation, with estimates suggesting that up to a million trees are cut down to meet demand.

By promoting sustainable practices and empowering youth through skill development, TFT GRIT is making a tangible impact on climate change and community development.
