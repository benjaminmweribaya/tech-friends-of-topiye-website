Welcome to Turkana Tech Youths Hub CBO

Empowering the youth of Turkana through skill development, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Our goal is to build a self-reliant and skilled community through TVET training and sustainable development initiatives.

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Turkana through quality Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET), fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and employability to drive community growth and improve livelihoods.

Our Vision

To build a self-reliant and skilled youth community in Turkana by leveraging TVET skills for sustainable development and socio-economic transformation.

Our Core Programs

Welding Training

Hands-on skills training in welding to enhance job opportunities for the youth.

Water Drilling Skills

Empowering youth with the skills necessary for water well drilling to support local water supply solutions.

Environmental Conservation

Promoting sustainable practices through tree planting and eco-friendly projects.

Innovative Projects

Smart Climate Stool (Ekicholong)

The Smart Climate Stool (Ekicholong), invented by our CEO Loroo, is made from scrap metal to help combat deforestation. This sustainable solution is spreading across Turkana and beyond, providing a durable and eco-friendly alternative to traditional wooden stools.
